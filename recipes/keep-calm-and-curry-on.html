Keep Calm and Curry On

How many curry jokes do I know? Honestly, Naan. But I do know how to make a good curry. Cumin and try it!

Ingredients

  • Spices that start with a "C" (cumin, cinnamon, coriander)
  • Fresh-squeezed Coconut Milk
  • Meat (optional)
  • Vegetables (optional)
  • Rice or Naan (optional)

Directions

Tikka the ingredients above and don't get curried away.

  1. Mix coconut milk with spices in pot
  2. Add in meat or vegetables
  3. Tell the pot to curry up and boil
  4. Serve with rice or naan if desired
