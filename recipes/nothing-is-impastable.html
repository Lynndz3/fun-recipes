Nothing is Impastable

Ready to spice up your daily rotini for pennes? This recipe is full of pastabilities.

Ingredients

  • Pasta!
  • Sauce
  • Meat (optional)
  • Parmesan (optional)

Directions

  2. Add pasta

  5. Feelin' cheesy, grate! Add parmesan.
  6. Tell your friends to "come and spaghet it!"
